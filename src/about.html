About The Author

My name is Francesca Ysabelle Galang. I am a Seneca Polytechnic Computer Programming and Analysis Student. For my WEB222 class, I have created a educational/informational website about one of my favourite Television Shows, The Owl House.

After researching online about The Owl House, I compiled my findings by writing a research summary. I used HTML5 markup to create this website and including a "class-less" CSS-stylesheet from https://css-tricks.com/no-class-css-frameworks/. I used the Water.css stylesheet for this website.

My website is about The Owl House because it is one of my favourite tv shows. I watched it with my brother and we both enjoyed watching it together. This show combines a lot of my interests into one really fun show! It has elements of magic, exciting world building, fantasy, comedy, drama, romance, friendship, and relatable characters. I loved going on the journey with Luz, the main character, as she discovers the fantastical world of "The Boiling Isles" and finally finds friends and a place where she feels like she truly belongs.
